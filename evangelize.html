About BBCYC

Bangladesh Baptist Church Youth Committee (BBCYC) was founded in the year 1974 and from its foundation it has been performing activities as an important wing of Bangladesh Baptist Church. From the period of its foundation it has been operating and organizing various types of religious, social, cultural and sports activities ensuring the involvement of youths.

Our Vision

The main vision of BBCYC is ensuring involvement of youth in various religious, social, cultural and sports activities in order to bring personal as well as social development. Aiming this vision BBCYC is organizing various events year-wide. As the youths are responsible for overall planning and execution of the events, these activities give birth to leadership skill among youths and that will lead the society to the constructive development.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a platform for the young minds of our Christian society to learn ways to build relationship with God and church, develop spiritual growth, practice leadership and extra-curricular skills so that they would be able to flourish successfully within the church and society. Moreover, BBCYC aims to increase mutual understanding & brotherhood through the events and activities.
